STEP 1 · FEATURE EXTRACTION
From spectrogram to constellation

The clip is converted to a time-frequency map (left) — brighter = louder at that frequency and moment. From that image, only the most prominent peaks are kept (right). Discarding amplitude keeps the fingerprint robust to volume changes, EQ, and mild noise.

STEP 2 · DATABASE SEARCH
Where in the song?

Full constellation of the matched song stored in the database. The highlighted window shows exactly where the query clip sits inside the full track.

STEP 3 · THE PROOF
The alignment spike

Every matched hash votes for a time offset (database frame − query frame). Chance matches scatter randomly — a genuine match makes them converge.
